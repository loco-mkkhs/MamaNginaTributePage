Mama Ngina was born Ngin Muhoho to Chief Muhoho wa Gathecha and Anne Nyokabi Muhoho at Ngenda in Kiambu district central province in 24th June 1933. She married Jomo Kenyatta as his fourth wife in 1951 and later became the First Lady of the Republic of Kenya in 1964 when Kenyatta became the First president of to 1978 when he died. She was a roman catholic member and used to attend church services with her children. She is the mother of four namely Uhuru Kenyatta, Muhoho Kenyatta, Christina Pratt and Anne Nyokabi Muthama. Mama Ngina is a step mother to Kenyatta's other three children, two by his first wife and one by the second.
